How to disable keyring for google-chrome

type "apt update && apt upgrade" hit enter ( internet required )
type "apt-get install seahorse" hit enter
and then terminal will ask you to confirm type "y" hit enter
once it's installed
open application tab search "pass"
and you will see "passwords and keys" click on it
in password section you will see "Login"
right click on it and then "change password"
a pop up window will appear it ask old password which is your linux password
type your password and hit enter
and then it will ask you for new password leave it blank 
and click on continue. it will say "store passwords unencrypted"
again click on continue and keyring will be disabled
open chrome and see the changes
