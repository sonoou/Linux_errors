one book but different footer for each chapter

1. Insert a section break at the end of chapter
  A. Place the cursor at the end of chapter 1
  B. Go to Layout -> Breaks -> Section Break(Next Page)

2. Unlink the footer from chapter 1
  A. click inside the footer of chapter 2 
     (first page of the chapter 2).
  B. In the Header & Footer Tools, find the option
     "Link to Previous" and disable it(uncheck it).

3. Update the footer for chapter 2
  A. Deleter "chapter 1" and replace it with "Chapter 2"
  B. Ensure the page number field remains unchanged
  C. The new footer will now apply only to chapter 2
